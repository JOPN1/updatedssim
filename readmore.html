OUR LEAD PASTOR

Pastor Nehemiah Esele Aluya is the founder and lead pastor of Streams of the Spirit International Ministry (SSIM), a ministry focused on raising supernatural people who are equipped with the knowledge of the gospel and knows how to walk with the spirit for kingdom advancement and influencing sectors for the father . A seasoned leadership and mentorship coach, Pastor Nehemiah is certified by the Miracle Invasion Leadership Institute and the Family Worship School of Mentorship and Leadership. His passion for empowering leaders inspired the creation of the School of Leadership and Mentorship (SLAM), a flagship initiative of SSIM. SLAM offers an intensive Leadership and Mentoring training to equip leaders, pastors, C-Suite Executives, career professionals, and entrepreneurs with practical strategies and spiritual insights to thrive in their fields He holds. Bachelor’s degree in Health Education from Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma, Edo State, Nigeria. He is happily married to Pastor Esther Nehemiah Aluya who serves alongside him in ministry. Together, they are devoted to transforming lives, nurturing leaders, and advancing the Kingdom of God both locally and globally.
